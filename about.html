I'm passionate about data science, machine learning, software development, pushups, all night hacking, and intense exercise. I am currently a data scientist and engineer at Sailthru. I've also worked at Adaptly, and Flat World Knowledge. I completed my BA in music, pre-med, and computer science at the liberal arts school, Bard College, in 2010. My professional interests include scientific computing, data science, statistics and machine learning, research and engineering. I don't have a PhD, but I'll earn one in a few years.

This site really is about me, so I hope you will take some time to peruse it. I've made an ongoing effort to present some of my projects and thoughts. You can also find me on github, thingiverse, twitter, stackoverflow, and linkedin!
