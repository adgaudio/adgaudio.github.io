I'm passionate about data science, machine learning, electronics, hardware, intense exercise and great food. My professional background is primarily in building software and analytics for data-focused startups. I also build physical machines for fun. I recently joined a small startup as the first employee after the cofounders, called Alluvium. Prior to that, I was a senior data scientist and engineer at Sailthru. I've also worked at Adaptly, and Flat World Knowledge. I completed my BA in music and pre-med in 2010 at the liberal arts college, Bard College.

I use this site to mention some of my projects and occassionally blog about interesting details behind them. You can also find me on github, thingiverse, twitter, stackoverflow, and linkedin!
